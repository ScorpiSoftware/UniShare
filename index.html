About Us

Here's a brief introduction to UniShare.

Our Mission


At UniShare, we believe that collaboration enhances learning. Our goal is to empower students by providing an intuitive platform where they can:
✅ Share coursework responsibly and efficiently.
✅ Leverage AI tools for writing, summarizing lectures, and creating study materials.
✅ Join online study sessions to collaborate with peers in real-time.
✅ Engage in a private, university-based community that fosters academic success.


Key Features


🎓 Coursework Sharing – Find and contribute class notes, assignments, and study materials.
🤖 AI-Powered Productivity – Generate practice tests, create flashcards, summarize lectures, and interact with PDFs.
📚 Group Study Sessions – Collaborate with classmates in real-time virtual study rooms.
🔍 University-Exclusive Access – A secure platform restricted to students from verified institutions.
📅 Personalized Dashboard – Track deadlines, coursework, and study sessions all in one place.


Why Choose UniShare?


Unlike traditional platforms, UniShare is built by students, for students. Our commitment is to:
🔒 Prioritize Privacy & Security – No public access, only verified university students.
🎯 Stay 100% Focused on Academics – No distractions, just pure productivity.
🤝 Foster Genuine Peer-to-Peer Learning – Helping students connect and learn from each other.
